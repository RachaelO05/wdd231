Non Profit Membership Level

Cost: Free

Benefits:

Chamber membership at no cost for registered non-profit organizations.

Listed in the online business directory.

Receive the monthly Chamber newsletter.

Access to networking events.

Invitations to educational workshops and community events.

Opportunities to collaborate with local businesses and organizations.

Bronze Membership Level

Bronze Membership

Cost: R500 per year

Benefits:

All Non-Profit Membership benefits.

Discounted registration for Chamber events.

Access to member-only business training sessions.

Business promotion through Chamber social media.

Opportunity to participate in Chamber committees.

Priority notification of upcoming networking opportunities.

Silver Membership Level

Silver Membership

Cost: R1,500 per year

Benefits:

All Bronze Membership benefits.

Featured business spotlight on the Chamber home page.

25% discount on Chamber-hosted events and workshops.

Complimentary admission to one premium networking event each year.

Access to exclusive business development seminars.

Gold Membership Level

Gold Membership

Cost: R3,000 per year

Benefits:

All Silver Membership benefits.

50% discount on Chamber events and training.

Featured business in the monthly Chamber newsletter.

Complimentary exhibitor booth at the annual Business Expo.

VIP invitations to exclusive networking breakfasts and leadership events.

Early access to sponsorship and partnership opportunities.

Dedicated business support from Chamber staff.
